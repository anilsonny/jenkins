Description:

When old age strikes, the legs of the elderly are often unable to bear the weight of the body and hence they require constant support to stand and walk. The case is the same with people who may have undergone injuries or surgeries in their legs. To help such people, Vissco Medipedic Tripod with Stick is a perfect product. The well designed L shape provides much needed support to the elderly and pacifies the difficulties they face in their everyday lives. The soft handle provides a firm and easy grip to the users. The length of the stick can also be adjusted to suit patients of all heights. The manufacturing process of the stick makes use of quite sturdy and lightweight material so as to make it feasible to be used by the patients. At the same time, the tripod at the bottom enables the stick to bear a lot of weight for a trouble free experience. At the same time, the anti-slip pod at the bottom of the stick provides an excellent grip on various kinds of surfaces.

Benefit of use:
  • Enables the elderly to walk around comfortably.
  • Helps people with leg injuries to stand and walk.
  • Anti slip pods make sure that there are no unannounced accidents on slippery surfaces like in the washroom.
  • The 3 leg design provides better stability than normal one legged sticks.
  • Helps to maintain the walking posture of senior citizens.
When to Use:
  • Can be used by the elderly who are suffering from weak legs due to old age.
  • Useful for patients who are suffering from leg injuries.
  • Also useful for sportpersons who are looking to rest their legs due to sore muscles.
  • Fit to be used during hip pain.
